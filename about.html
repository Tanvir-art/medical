Our Caring For You

A perfect match

Every patient has a distinct personality so caregiving is not a one size fits all profession. It’s why all of our patients receive an individualized care plan and why we use our signature aide pairing process to match patients with the right aides. It’s also why we provide ongoing training and skill development, so our teams of caregivers possess the techniques needed to assist you and your loved ones.

The right care

ETo ensure our patients get the care they need, we send a registered nurse to the home to do a comprehensive assessment. A care plan is then developed and carried out by the caregiver.

Caregivers that speak your languag

We have a diverse staff that speak a broad range of languages. We do our best to pair patients with caregivers who speak their native language. Languages we can accomodate include: English, Español, Pусский, Français, Creole ,Cantonese, Fujianese, Gujarati, Hebrew,Hindi, Mandarin

Managing the paperwork

Our internal team manages the insurance companies and the accompanying paperwork so our patients don’t have to. If you don’t have a referral we can get you one and we’ll file all the paperwork. We work with most New York MLTC, CHHA & hospice companies

Leaders in the field of care

Every member of the Amazing Home Care family is a top-notch professional with the education and training necessary to ensure quality care. They are guided by a team of dedicated leaders, providing support and ensuring premium care at every step.
