Our Services

Home Health Aides

All of our aides are carefully vetted, certified and extensively trained to ensure quality of care. We’ll match you with someone who understands your needs and can help with activities of daily living.

CDPAP

Choose a family member or friend to care for you. Medicaid recipients can choose their own caregiver through New York’s Consumer Directed Personal Assistance Program (CDPAP). Amazing Home Care is an experienced fiscal intermediary (FI) and provides the support needed to get a caregiver through CDPAP.

Waiver Programs

Waiver programs provide services to those who would otherwise be medically eligible for placement in a hospital or nursing facility for an extended period of time if such programs were unavailable. Patients receive case management and may receive other services based on assessment and need
